team sports

Any activity in which individuals are organized into opposing teams that compete to win or collaborate to entertain their audience is considered a team sport. Team members work together to achieve a common goal. This can be accomplished in a variety of methods, including outscoring the opposing team. To achieve their aims, team members establish goals, make choices, communicate, manage conflict, and solve problems in a supportive, trusting environment.

Baseball

Cricket

Rugby
